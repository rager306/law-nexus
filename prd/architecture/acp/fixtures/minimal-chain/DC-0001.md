# DC-0001 — Require fixture validation before ACP runtime adoption

## Candidate decision

Adopt the rule that ACP runtime/dashboard/registry integration should wait until a minimal fixture chain is validated and a derived recovery view can be produced from source records.

## Current status

Candidate only. This is not an accepted architecture decision yet.

## Why it matters

Without this candidate, future agents may jump from research artifacts directly to broad tooling. The safer path is a tiny mechanics proof with clear blocked actions and no product readiness claims.
